# Contributor Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming environment, contributors
and maintainers pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free
experience for everyone.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language.
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Gracefully accepting constructive feedback.
- Focusing on what is best for the community and users.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, insults, or exclusionary conduct.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying acceptable behavior and may
remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, and issues that are not aligned
with this Code of Conduct.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it to the maintainer:

- Philipp Wegmüller <philipp.wegmueller@seco.admin.ch>

All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
